Aloo Matar

Description

Aloo Matar is a classic North Indian comfort food. It is a delicious curry made with potatoes (aloo) and green peas (matar) cooked in a spiced onion-tomato sauce. It pairs perfectly with roti, naan, or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed or finely chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ½ teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook this masala until the oil starts to separate from the sides.
  4. Add the potato cubes and green peas. Stir well to coat them with the masala.
  5. Pour in the water and bring the curry to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for 15–20 minutes until the potatoes are tender.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and fresh coriander leaves on top. Mix well and serve hot.
